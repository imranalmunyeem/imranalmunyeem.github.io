About

Researcher-engineer working at the intersection of cybersecurity and software quality.

My academic and professional work sits at the intersection of cybersecurity, software engineering, AI-assisted testing, and cyber-physical systems. I am particularly interested in how secure, realistic, and scalable cyber training environments can improve cyber resilience in academic and industrial contexts.

Before starting my PhD, I completed an MSc in Software Engineering and Applications with Distinction from the University of Bedfordshire and worked across software quality assurance, test automation, API testing, CI/CD, performance testing, and security assessment.

Research Focus

Cybersecurity research with practical engineering depth.

Cyber Range Architectures

Designing hybrid cyber range environments that combine on-premises, virtualised, containerised, and cloud infrastructures for realistic cybersecurity experimentation and training.

Cyber-Physical Systems Security

Investigating secure and scalable approaches for modelling, testing, and training within cyber-physical environments where digital and physical systems interact.

AI-Assisted Security Testing

Exploring how AI and large language models can support test generation, vulnerability analysis, scenario design, and security automation workflows.

DevSecOps & Quality Engineering
